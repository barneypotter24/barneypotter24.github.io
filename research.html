Research

Senior Thesis

I am currently developing a definition and implementation for the Prize-Collecting Steiner Tree in directed signaling hypergraphs. The long-term goal of this project is to develop a methodology for hypothesis generation using known cell-signaling interactions, and empirical expression data. This would allow researchers to generate hypotheses about upstream cell-signaling processes that may contribute to diseases such as cancer or schizophrenia.

Project Megadopsis

I am working on a project to determine the immediate (first few generations) effects of whole genome duplication on the size and content of Arabidopsis thaliana with Dr. Jeremy Coate. Additionally, I will be using the differential expression data that I have already generated to develop weighting schema for the directed hypergraphs that I am using for my thesis.
